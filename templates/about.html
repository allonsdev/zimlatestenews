About Us

Zimbabwe's most trusted independent news source since 2025.

Our Mission

To inform, educate and empower Zimbabweans at home and in the diaspora with credible, fearless journalism.

Editorial Standards

We follow strict editorial guidelines to ensure every story is verified before publication. Corrections are published promptly and transparently.

Our Team

Our newsroom is staffed by experienced journalists based in Harare, Bulawayo and across the country.

Where We Are

Headquartered in Harare, Zimbabwe, with correspondents covering all provinces and the Zimbabwean diaspora worldwide.

Our Story

ZimLatestNews was founded in 2025 with a single purpose: to give Zimbabweans a reliable, independent and digital first news platform they could trust. In a media landscape often shaped by political and commercial pressures, we set out to do things differently.

We cover politics, business, sport, health, technology and entertainment with the same commitment to accuracy and fairness. Every story we publish is held to the same standard regardless of who it involves.

Today ZimLatestNews reaches hundreds of thousands of readers every month across Zimbabwe and the diaspora. We remain proudly independent and committed to the public interest.
